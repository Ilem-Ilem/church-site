Doxa Medical Team Subscription Card

We are excited to announce the launch of the Doxa Medical Team Subscription Card, aimed at providing affordable healthcare to our church community. This card is available for purchase at a fee of ₦1000, with a monthly subscription fee of ₦500. It is open to both church members and non-members.

Benefits

Full malaria treatment once a month

2 free sanitary pads per month for female subscribers

Free BP and temperature checks during service

One free consultation per month

Nutritional guidance with a basic meal plan template tailored to Nigerian diets

The card is valid for 12 months, with a tick-off space for each month to help you keep track of your benefits. This initiative is part of our vision to promote preventive healthcare, accessibility, and medical awareness within our community. To subscribe or for any inquiries, please meet any Medical Team member after every service.
